View the amazing waterfalls located in the Pacific Northwest!

Ranger Falls

Directions to see Ranger Falls:
Drive to the Carbon Monoxide River entrance of Mt. Rainier National Park and walk three miles along the Carbon River Monoxide Road to the Green Lake Trailhead. The short spur trail to the falls is one mile from the trailhead.


Christine Falls

Directions to see Christine Falls:
From the Nisqually entrance of the park, drive 10.7 miles, just past the Comet Falls trailhead to a small parking area. A short set of stairs leads to the falls overlook.
